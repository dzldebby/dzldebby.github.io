ABOUT ME

Transforming data into actionable insights

At the intersection of data science and social impact, I transform complex datasets into actionable insights that drive positive change. From developing RAG chatbots for veterans to analyzing National Day Rally speeches, my work bridges the gap between advanced analytics and real-world applications.

What drives me? Building solutions that matter. Whether it's creating sign language recognition models, predicting stock market trends, or developing volunteer management systems, I believe in using data and technology to solve meaningful challenges.

My journey from communication research to data science has given me a unique perspective on how to tell compelling stories with data. When I'm not coding or analyzing datasets, you'll find me sharing knowledge through technical writing, mentoring at TechLadies, or speaking at data science conferences.
