Journey

M y path in data science has been an exciting adventure of continuous learning and discovery. It all started with a curiosity about how numbers could tell compelling stories about human behavior and societal trends. This curiosity led me from the world of communication research to the dynamic field of data analytics.

What gets me excited? Those 'aha!' moments when a complex analysis suddenly reveals a clear insight.

The collaborative energy of hackathons where diverse minds come together to solve challenges. I believe in the importance of ethical data practices and in making data insights accessible to everyone. When I'm not diving into datasets or crafting visualizations, you might find me mentoring aspiring data scientists or contributing to open-source projects.
